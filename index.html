About Me

Passionate designer with a fresh perspective and hunger to learn

I’m a passionate UI/UX designer and creative thinker with a background in Computer Applications. My work blends design, technology, and storytelling to create intuitive digital experiences that make a real impact. With skills in Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, and UX strategy, I enjoy crafting user-friendly solutions — from mental health support platforms to innovative mobile apps. Beyond design, I love exploring ideas through painting, reading, and badminton, which inspire creativity in my projects.

My journey in design is driven by curiosity and a genuine desire to solve problems through thoughtful design. I believe great design happens when we truly understand user needs and translate them into intuitive, beautiful interfaces that make people's lives easier and more enjoyable.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me exploring the latest design trends, experimenting with new tools, or working on personal projects that challenge my creativity and push my boundaries as a designer.
