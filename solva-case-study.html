SOLVA - Mental Health & Wellness App

A comprehensive mobile application designed to connect users with verified therapists and provide essential tools for mental wellness tracking.

Project Overview

SOLVA is a mental health and wellness app designed to provide users with accessible mental health support through verified therapists and comprehensive wellness tracking tools. The project focused on creating an intuitive, calming, and accessible user experience.

Design Process

The design process began with extensive user research to understand the needs and pain points of individuals seeking mental health support. We conducted interviews, created user personas, and developed user journeys to ensure the app met real user needs.

Key Features

  • Therapist matching and booking system
  • Mood tracking and journaling
  • Meditation and mindfulness exercises
  • Progress tracking and insights
  • Secure messaging with therapists

Challenges & Solutions

One of the main challenges was creating a design that felt both professional and comforting. We achieved this through a carefully selected color palette, intuitive navigation, and accessibility-focused design principles.
